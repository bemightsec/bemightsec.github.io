About BeMight

Learning cybersecurity with discipline, clarity, and responsibility.

Prince Awuku Anobaah, known professionally as BeMight, is building a practical path into cybersecurity through structured learning, defensive security labs, and clear documentation. The goal is to develop skills that help people and organisations understand, detect, and reduce digital risk.

Professional Direction

Junior cybersecurity analyst path

BeMight’s current direction is blue team and defensive security, with interest in threat detection, network traffic analysis, cybersecurity awareness, OSINT basics, and practical documentation. The portfolio is also open to internships, mentorship, beginner security projects, and feedback from professionals.
